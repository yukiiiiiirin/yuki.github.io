WORK EXPERIENCE

R & D Engineer, Hubei Chude Pharmaceutical Co., Ltd. (10/2023-Present)

  • Led research on drug-carrier matrices and safety for medical patches, mainly using bioresponsive hydrogels to ensure targeted drug release and optimize the release profiles of APIs, followed by safety and biocompatibility assessments.
  • Improved manufacturing processes and quality control for stability and safety in large-scale production.
  • Collaborated with other departments to set product development goals and strategies, evaluating technical feasibility.
  • Oversaw patent applications, prepared product registration documentation, and supported product launches.

R & D Assistant Engineer, Angel Yeast Co., Ltd (06/2022-05/2023)

  • Engaged in research on the biosynthesis of the novel photodynamic drug ALA.
  • Studied the reconstruction and optimization of yeast metabolic pathways through gene knockout, heterologous gene expression, regulatory elements, etc., to increase the yield or alter metabolic direction.
  • Managed to reduce glycerol production in alcohol fermentation and engineered recombinant yeast to express protease activity based on metabolic product analysis, thus enhancing yeast utilization of protein in raw material liquids.

PROJECT EXPERIENCE

Construction and Characterization of the Engineering Pichia Pastoris with Co-expressing Adhesin and Antimicrobial Peptide (09/2019-06/2022)

  • Constructed a recombinant yeast using Pichia pastoris surface display technology and secretion expression system for co-displaying of adhesion and antimicrobial peptides without mutual interference.
  • Reached the optimal expression efficiency of anchor proteins through flow cytometry fluorescence intensity analysis, surface hydrophobicity measurement, and Zeta potential measurement.
  • Achieved tandem gene expression of adhesins in vitro and electrotransformed them into recombinant yeast.
  • Validated the co-expressing recombinant yeast through immunofluorescence microscopy and tested its inhibition of targeted pathogenic bacteria and cell adhesion.

Isolation, Identification, and Characterization Analysis of a Salt-Tolerant Staphylococcus Strain (08/2020-01/2022)

  • Isolated, identified, and characterized the salt-tolerant staphylococcus strain SW-1 through morphological, physiological, and biochemical experiments, 16S rDNA gene sequencing, and bioevolutionary tree analysis.
  • Tested the bacteria for drug sensitivity, salt tolerance, and surface hydrophobicity to identify desired strains.

Preparation and Application of a Recombinant Yeast Co-displaying Three NSP Enzymes (09/2019-06/2021)

  • Prepared high-activity whole cell catalysts via flask fermentation after creating recombinant microbial systems.
  • Improved and verified the great synergistic promoting effect on the degradation of NSP in wheat bran.

Construction of the Thermostable Xylanase Whole-Cell Biocatalyst Based on Yeast Cell Surface Display Technology and Enzymatic Analyses (09/2019-01/2021)

  • Optimized yeast surface display for enzyme presentation, characterized and verified enzyme display efficiency.
  • Conducted comparative analysis of the enzymological properties of whole cell catalyst and free enzyme.
  • Evaluated the catalytic performance of yeast-displayed xylanase under various conditions for quality control.

Study on Enrichment and Purification Technology of Total Flavonoids from Lotus Leaves and Apocynum Venetum L. (09/2017-04/2019)

  • Screened macroporous resin based on static adsorption and desorption rates to investigate its dynamic adsorption.
  • Resorted single factor test and orthogonal test to optimize the purification conditions of total flavonoids.
  • Conducted chromatographic separations, solvent system assessments, extraction condition improvements, and analyzed the composition and bioactivity of the purified flavonoid fractions.
